Emergency Go Bag Essentials



First Aid Kit


Prepare a medkit with bandages, antispetics, gauze, medical wipes, disposable gloves, pain relivers, etc.

Food & Water


Pack at least 3 days worth of non-perishable food and at least 1 gallon of water per person. Store in a cool and safe area, away from chemicals.

Important documents


Keep copies of important documents in ziplock bags or sealed plastic envelopes. Include birth certificates, passports, ids, medical records, and allergy lists.

Cash


Keep a small, secure stash of cash for emergencies when electronic payments aren't available.
